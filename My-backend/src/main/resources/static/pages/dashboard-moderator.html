Moderation Guidelines

Guidelines and policies for moderating animal submissions.

Moderation Checklist
When reviewing submissions, check for:
  • Accuracy: Verify scientific names and information
  • Completeness: Ensure all required fields are filled
  • Relevance: Confirm the animal is native to the Philippines
  • Sources: Check if credible sources are cited
  • Image Quality: Ensure images are clear and relevant
Action Guidelines:
APPROVE (Filter): When submission is factual, complete, and follows guidelines
REJECT: When submission has false information, is incomplete, or violates policies
Common Issues to Watch For:
  • Duplicate entries of existing animals
  • Incorrect conservation status
  • Poor quality or irrelevant images
  • Plagiarized content
  • Inappropriate or off-topic submissions
Quick Reference
Important Notes
  • Always provide clear notes when rejecting
  • Be respectful in all communications
  • Flag suspicious activity to admins
  • Follow escalation procedures for disputes

For complex issues or questions, contact the admin team.
