About Animalphidia

Animalphidia is a comprehensive digital encyclopedia dedicated to documenting and preserving information about Philippine wildlife. Our mission is to educate, inspire conservation efforts, and provide a platform for wildlife enthusiasts and researchers.

Conservation Spotlight

Why Conservation Matters

The Philippines is one of the world's biodiversity hotspots, but its wildlife faces numerous threats including habitat loss, climate change, and illegal wildlife trade. Our conservation efforts focus on protecting endangered species and their habitats.
